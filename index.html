Premature Elaculation

In males, premature ejaculation happens when sperm leaves the body (ejaculates) earlier than desired during intercourse. It is a frequent sexual issue and most of the people call I as a premature ejaculation. One in in three persons reports having it at some point.

Premature ejaculation is not a reason for worry if it occurs seldom. However, you may be diagnosed with premature ejaculation if you do the following:

  • Within 1 to 3 minutes of penetration, always or nearly always ejaculate.
  • Are unable to defer ejaculation entirely or nearly entirely during intercourse.
  • Feel disturbed and irritated, and as a result, avoid physical closeness.
